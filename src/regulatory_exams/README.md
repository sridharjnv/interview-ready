# Regulatory Body Exams - India (2025-2026)

## 📋 Overview

## 📝 Key Observations

1. **RBI Grade B** offers the highest salary (~₹32-42 Lakhs CTC)
2. **IRDAI Grade A** has higher CTC than RBI Grade A despite lower basic pay
3. **SEBI and RBI Grade A** now have revised pay scales with ₹62,500 basic pay (2025 revision)
4. **NABARD, SIDBI, PFRDA, IBBI, NHB** still follow the ₹44,500 basic pay structure
5. **FSSAI** follows Central Government pay scales, resulting in significantly lower salaries
